## How this archive is organized

Everything the desk publishes lands here first. Each volume groups a quarter's worth of work — the ranking that anchors the issue, the field notes that support it, and the market guides that give buyers and sellers context beyond a simple top-10 list. Volumes are numbered in descending order, newest first, so the most current ranking is always at the top of the page.

Within a volume, entries are tagged by type — Realtor Index, Field Note, or Market Guide — so a reader chasing one specific thread can follow it across issues without re-reading the whole archive. Every entry links back to the live page it belongs to, so the archive stays a map of the work rather than a duplicate of it.

## Using the archive for research

Buyers, sellers, and agents themselves use this page differently. A buyer weighing two agents on the current Realtor Index can trace each profile back to the field notes and market guides that informed the score, rather than taking a ranking at face value.

Journalists and other outlets citing the Index are welcome to link directly to individual entries here; each item has a stable anchor so a specific ranking, field note, or market guide can be referenced without pointing to a moving target.
